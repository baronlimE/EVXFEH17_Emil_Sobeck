Midnight AB

Midnight AB was a ISP and network design company in the late 1990's. The company consisted of three co-founders and some project employes. The main issue of business was to establish a First On Scene foothold as ISP in the Kaliningrad conclave. Midnight managed to establish a joint-venture sub company with a local hardware/software support business firm. The main customers consisted of the university, a bank with sub offices, the county administrative department and a company-park that housed mainly european companies and firms.
